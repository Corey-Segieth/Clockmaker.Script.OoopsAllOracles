# Ooops All Oracles
## Characters
* ORacle?: Each night, learn 2 players at least one of which is good.
* NORacle: Each night, learn 2 players that are not evil.
* NANDacle: Each night, learn 2 players at least one of which is evil.
* XORacle: Each night, learn 2 players which do not share an alignment.
* XNORacle: Each night, learn 2 players which share an alignment.
* Dooracle: Each night, learn 2 players with evil nieghbors.
* Devouracle: Each Night, learn how many pairs of evil players there are.
* Clocracle: Each night, learn how many steps the is Demon from its nearest Minion.
* Packle: Each Night, learn how many pairs of good players there are.
* Empathacle: Each night, you learn how many of your nirghbors are evil.
* Stackle: Each night, you learn how many steps you are from the nearest demon.
* Moracle: Each night*, you learn how many "oracles" (Townsfolk) are alive.
* Ordinary Oracle: Each night, you learn how many dead players are evil.
* Lunatic: You think you are a Demon, but you are not. The Demon knows who you are & who you choose at night.
* Drunk: You do not know you are the Drunk. You think you are a Townsfolk character, but you are not.
* Recluse: You might register as evil & as a Minion or Demon, even if dead.
* Plague Doctor: When you die, the Storyteller gains a Minion ability.
* Spy: Each night, you see the Grimoire. You might register as good & as a Townsfolk or Outsider, even if dead.
* Marionette: You think you are a good character, but you are not. The Demon knows who you are. [You neighbor the Demon]
* Scarlet Woman: If there are 5 or more players alive & the Demon dies, you become the Demon. (Travellers don't count.)
* Xaan: On night X, all Townsfolk are poisoned until dusk. [X Outsiders]
* Leviathan: If more than 1 good player is executed, evil wins. All players know you are in play. After day 5, evil wins.
* Tor-acle: It is just Normal Tor. Players don't know their character or alignment. They learn them after they die.
